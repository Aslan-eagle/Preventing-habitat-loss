Factors that cause habitat loss

Habitat loss is one of the global issues. In fact, if we don’t take any action, this might cause more harsh consequences such as, extinction of different species of animals.One of the causes is pollution. Pollution is another global issue which is causing global warming. Nowadays, half of the population of the world isn’t aware of the consequences of pollution to our world. In fact, this might cause different species of fish to get extinct. This is because of water pollution such as, when people are littering the ocean, lakes with plastics. As a result, pollution causes habitat loss. Another cause is poaching. Approximately 10-15,000 are killed each year by poachers. This leads to destruction of habitat and extinction of animals.

How mining causes habitatloss?

One of the causes of habitat destruction is mining because it distrupts the ecosystem, biomes. In the progress of mining, wearing away of earth, it destroys landscapes, forests, wildlife habitats, plants, etc. This is because before the mining, they are cleared out from the territory. Thus, strip mining causes soil erosion and destruction of agricultural land. Another negative effect is the pollution. When rain washes the soil into rivers, streams, the sediments, materials from the mining pollute the whole area, waterways. This causes the extinction of fishes, plant life destruction, and flooding because there will be damage to river channels. Moreover, it causes dust and noise pollution which occurs when the soil is disrupted with heavy machines used for mining and when coal dust is created due to mining works. As an example, the current rate of deforestation is 160,000 square kilometers per year due to mining. Moreover, according to research by Keith Veronese, “By the year 2016, the population of China is projected to consume 130,000 tons of rare earth metals a year”. In fact, this is a large amount of rare earth metals which demonstrates the fact that China has done a lot of mining which had tons of negative impact on habitats, earth, environment, extinction of different types of species, etc.
